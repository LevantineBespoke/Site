Levantine Bespoke

Handcrafted Objects & Timeless Heritage

Bridging Antiquity and Modern Architecture

Commissioned exclusively for elite interior design houses, Levantine Bespoke elevates the ancient art of Damascene nacre inlay into clean, modern forms. Every piece is a singular masterwork of craftsmanship, meticulously built to serve as a focal point in the world's most considered spaces.

We build heirloom-quality bespoke statement pieces defined by their uncompromising raw materials and absolute structural integrity. Operating strictly as a bespoke workshop, our capabilities range from intimate collector's boxes to large-scale custom furniture and architectural paneling, built exactly to your specifications.

Solid Walnut

Cut from seasoned, heavyweight espresso walnut. We utilize strictly traditional mortise and tenon joinery, ensuring lifetime structural integrity without the use of industrial fasteners.

Raw Sadaf

Thick-cut, iridescent mother-of-pearl (Sadaf), hand-chiseled and pressure-set into the timber. The natural topography of the shell is preserved to interact dynamically with ambient light.

Hand-Applied Finish

We avoid industrial lacquers in favor of highly detailed, hand-applied finishing techniques. Each piece is repeatedly polished to achieve a refined, durable luster that enhances the natural depth of the walnut and the iridescence of the nacre.

International Trade & Logistics
